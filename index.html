WITH HEBA KADRY

FREE ARTICLE

Heba Kadry carved her own way to the top. After years spent travelling, studying and working in high-end studios, the Egyptian engineer developed an artistic approach to mastering that caught the attention of many of the world's most inventive artists, including Björk, Slowdive, Ryuichi Sakamoto, Animal Collective and more.

Heba joins us from her beautifully-crafted studio in Dumbo, New York, to tell us more about her journey and her mastering approach.

ROSALÍA

WITH JAYCEN JOSHUA

Originally submitted as a university assignment, Rosalía's second album went on to become one of the most important cultural works of the 2010s. El Mal Querer is a fearless odyssey into the world's of flamenco, pop and hip hop, with remarkable songs that touched millions of hearts around the globe. Jaycen Joshua, takes us inside the mix of the album's breakout hit, "Malamente", demonstrating how his signature low-end and transient control contributed towards making one of the decade's most influential songs.

STORY OF A BOND THEME

FREE ARTICLE

The latest original song to soundtrack the James Bond universe, "No Time To Die", is a masterstroke in songwriting, performance, production and mixing. This feature examines the entire process, featuring interviews with Finneas O’Connell, Hans Zimmer, Stephen Lipson and Rob Kinelski.
